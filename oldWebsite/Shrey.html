General things that you need to know about Html.

Html(hypertext markup language)

Html is what is used to make the outline of your website. These words that you are reading now were coded in html. Html coding is made of tags. Tags are tells the html interpretor, what they are reading should look like. Tags are organized in sets of 2; an opening tag and a closing tag. The opening tag will have a: less than sign, the type of tag(see roger's section for types of tags), and then a greater than sign. A closing tag will have: a less than sign, a forward slash, the type of tag, and then a greater than tag.
