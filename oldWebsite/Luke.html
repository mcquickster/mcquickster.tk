The history of html


I don't know that much about the history of html but I probably know more than eveyone else in this class room.


Html(the original)was the first Internet-based language developed strictly for the web. It was created from binary. The company that made the original html language also made html 2-4. Then it went out of buissness and was replaced by xhtml because html was to limited and gliched too often for what professional coders needed. Xhtml(a seperate company) was supposed to fix all of the problems with html but it only survived 2 generations. Durring the era of xhtml some teenagers were developing a new coding language and they called it html5. (I don't know why they didn't violate copyright.) They just finished it before xhtml2 went out of buissness. Then they released it. It was a huge success. Most all of the professional coders used it. We still use it today!
